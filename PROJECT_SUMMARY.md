# Qalin Sara Inventory Management System

## Project Summary for Collaborators & Partners

---

### üéØ The Problem

Small and medium-sized carpet retailers face significant challenges in managing their inventory efficiently. Traditional methods‚Äîspreadsheets, paper records, or generic retail software‚Äîfail to address the unique needs of carpet businesses:

- **Size Variants**: Carpets come in multiple sizes (2m to 12m), each requiring separate stock tracking and pricing
- **High-Value Inventory**: Carpets are expensive items requiring precise value tracking for financial planning
- **Visual Identification**: Buyers need to see product photos; text descriptions alone are insufficient
- **Multi-Currency Operations**: Many carpet businesses import internationally and sell locally

These challenges lead to stock discrepancies, missed sales opportunities, and inefficient business operations.

---

### üí° The Solution

**Qalin Sara** is a modern, web-based inventory management system purpose-built for carpet retailers. It provides:

| Feature | Benefit |
|---------|---------|
| **Multi-Size Tracking** | Track 6 size variants per product with individual pricing and stock levels |
| **Visual Catalog** | Photo uploads for every product with preview capabilities |
| **Order Management** | Create, track, and fulfill customer orders with automatic calculations |
| **Invoice Generation** | Professional invoices with PDF export and payment tracking |
| **Expense Tracking** | Monitor business expenses with categorization and receipt storage |
| **Real-Time Analytics** | Dashboard with inventory value, stock alerts, and business metrics |
| **Multi-Language Support** | 8 languages including English, German, French, Arabic, and Pashto |

---
